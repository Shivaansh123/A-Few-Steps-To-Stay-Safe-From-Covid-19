How To Stay Safe From COVID 19

~ Made by Shivaansh Tirumala.

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30
seconds will help you avoid germs.

Cover Your Mouth & Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or
coughing in public. Even better use a mask.

Stay Home Stay Safe Avoid

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places. Prefer
staying indoors.

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to keep
your self calm and composed.
